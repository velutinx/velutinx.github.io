Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VELUTINX (the "Bot") collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What Data We Collect

The Bot collects the following types of data to provide its core features:

1.1 Discord Account Data

  • Discord User ID: To uniquely identify you and associate your votes, game progress, and activity.
  • Discord Username & Tag: For display purposes and logging.
  • Server Membership: Which servers you are in where the Bot is active.
  • Roles: Your server roles to calculate vote weights and assign permissions.

1.2 Activity Data

  • Poll Votes: Your selections, voting weight, and timestamps.
  • Game Progress: Wins, losses, scores, and participation in games (Wordle, Hangman, Trivia, etc.).
  • Message Activity: Message counts for XP and level tracking.
  • Ticket Balances: Current and historical ticket counts earned through activity.

1.3 Technical Data

  • IP Addresses: For website poll votes and verification (stored anonymously).
  • Interaction Timestamps: When you interact with the Bot.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate and display weighted poll results.
  • To award and track tickets based on activity.
  • To assign server roles based on membership status.
  • To improve the Bot and its features.
  • To moderate server activity and prevent abuse.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in a secure Cloudflare D1 database.
  • Access to the database is restricted to authorized personnel and systems.
  • We use industry‑standard encryption and security practices to protect your data.
  • No sensitive personal information (e.g., passwords, financial data) is stored by the Bot.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with service providers (e.g., Cloudflare for database hosting) solely to operate the Bot.
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as you actively use the Bot.
  • If you stop using the Bot, we may retain your data for up to 30 days before deletion.
  • Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Restriction: You can request restriction of processing in certain cases.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us (see Section 8).

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Contact Information

For privacy‑related questions, concerns, or data deletion requests:

  • Discord: VELUTINX Community Server (DM velutinxx)
  • Email: support@velutinx.com
  • Website: velutinx.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice in the Bot's server or via the Bot itself. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
